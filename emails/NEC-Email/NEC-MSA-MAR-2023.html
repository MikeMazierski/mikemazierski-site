Master of Science in Accounting

Advanced education that builds upon your career experience.
      Learn How      

The ability to think critically is important for accountants who seek to advance their careers through strategic insight into risk analysis, auditing, taxation, and other issues. That’s why New England College’s online Master of Science in Accounting degree program is designed to provide a broader perspective that also helps refine your analysis and problem-solving skills.

Reasons to choose New England College:
•

Learn 100% online with flexible scheduling for busy professionals
•

Graduate with your degree in as few as 2 years
•

Specialize your degree in either general or forensic accounting
•

Prepare for professional certification programs

Based on the business competencies identified by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, the MSA program satisfies the 150-hour requirement for the CPA examination in most states. This strong curricular foundation can provide you with a path to certification, creating opportunity in a variety of established and well-respected fields.
